Privacy Policy

Clear language for how SoTree handles information.

Last updated: June 5, 2026

SoTree is a privacy-conscious social media archive, analytics, capture, and account management workspace. This policy explains the difference between social-media information that stays in the app and limited account or subscription metadata that may be used to support access to the service.

Core privacy position

Social-media archive files, social account details inside those archives, and related analysis stay in the app. SoTree does not collect that social-media information.

Contact

Questions about privacy or data handling can be sent to alaka.softwares@gmail.com.

This policy applies as of June 5, 2026.

Information SoTree may use

  • Account information used for authentication, such as email address, account identifiers, and sign-in metadata.
  • Subscription and entitlement information used to determine access to paid features and usage limits.
  • User preferences and app settings, such as workspace options, product limits, and feature state.
  • SoTree does not collect your social-media archive files, social account details from those archives, or the related analysis content generated inside the app.

How the information is used

  • To create and maintain your account.
  • To verify subscription access and manage plan entitlements.
  • To store app preferences, enforce product limits, troubleshoot service issues, and improve service reliability.
  • Social-media archive processing and analysis happen within the app experience without SoTree collecting that social-media content.

Third-party services

  • Firebase or other Google services may be used for authentication and related app infrastructure.
  • RevenueCat may be used to manage subscription status and entitlement access.
  • Apple and Google Play billing services may process purchases, renewals, and related billing records where applicable.

Important privacy statements

  • SoTree does not sell personal data.
  • Social-media archive content, social account details in those archives, and related analysis stay in the app and are not collected by SoTree.
  • Account, subscription, and app-configuration metadata may still be used through services such as Firebase, RevenueCat, and app-store billing where needed to provide access and entitlement controls.
  • You should only import archives you own or have rights to process.

Retention and deletion

Retention may vary depending on the type of information, the features you use, account status, operational needs, and legal obligations. You can review deletion options on the Data Deletion page.

Children and legal scope

SoTree is not intended to authorize misuse of platform data, harassment, or unlawful conduct. If local laws require additional disclosures, update this policy before publishing in those jurisdictions.
