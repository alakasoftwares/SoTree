Archive Guides

General steps for downloading your own social media archives.

Platform interfaces change often. These guides stay intentionally general so you can find the right export flow even if menu labels move.

Important note

Only import archives you own or have rights to use. If platform screens change, check that platform's official help documentation for the latest instructions.

Private by design

These archives are meant for in-app use inside SoTree. Social-media archive content and related account details are not collected by SoTree and do not leave the app.

X / Twitter archive

  1. Open X settings.
  2. Go to Your account or the current account management section.
  3. Find the option to download an archive of your data.
  4. Complete any identity verification the platform requests.
  5. Submit the archive request.
  6. Download the ZIP file when X says it is ready.

Facebook archive

  1. Open Accounts Center or Facebook settings.
  2. Find the information and permissions area.
  3. Choose the option to download your information.
  4. Select the profile, date range, media quality, and file format you want.
  5. Submit the request and download the ZIP once it is prepared.

Instagram archive

  1. Open Accounts Center or Instagram settings.
  2. Find the information and permissions section.
  3. Choose the option to download your information.
  4. Select the Instagram profile you want to export.
  5. Request the archive and download the ZIP when it becomes available.
