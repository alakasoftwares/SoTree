FAQ

Questions people usually ask first

Does SoTree sell personal data?

No. SoTree does not sell personal data. Social-media archive content is not collected by SoTree and stays in the app.

Does my social-media information leave the app?

No. Social-media archive content, account details from those archives, and in-app analysis remain in the app. Separate account or subscription infrastructure may still handle login or entitlement metadata.

Is SoTree affiliated with X or Meta?

No. SoTree is an independent archive, analytics, capture, and account management workspace.

Do I need my own archive?

Yes. You should only import data you own or have rights to process. The archive guide explains the general export steps.

What does Premium change?

Premium may expand analytics depth, import capacity, session limits, and capture tools. Exact feature packaging may evolve over time.
