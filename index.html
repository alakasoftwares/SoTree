Privacy-First Promise

Designed around careful handling, not data extraction.

SoTree is designed around privacy-conscious archive analysis. Imported archive content and related analysis are processed inside the app experience and are not sold by SoTree. Limited account, subscription, and app metadata may still be processed through Firebase, RevenueCat, and app-store billing services where needed.

Only import archives you own or have permission to use. SoTree is an analysis and organization workspace, not an official platform partner.

FAQ

Questions people usually ask first

Does SoTree sell personal data?

No. SoTree does not sell personal data or use imported social-media archives for advertising.

Does my social-media information leave the app?

SoTree is designed so imported archive content and related analysis are handled inside the app experience. Separate account, subscription, and app infrastructure may still process limited metadata for login, access control, billing, and product limits.

Is SoTree affiliated with X or Meta?

No. SoTree is an independent archive, analytics, capture, and account management workspace.

Do I need my own archive?

Yes. You should only import data you own or have rights to process. The archive guide explains the general export steps.

What does Premium change?

Premium may expand analytics depth, import capacity, session limits, and capture tools. Exact feature packaging may evolve over time.
